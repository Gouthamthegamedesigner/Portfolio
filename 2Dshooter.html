2D Shooter (Name not finalised)

2D Shooter is a 2D casual game for Android.
Main player is a 2D cube with a gun.
The player stands in a world with different platforms. And player is being attacked by cubes of different sizes. Player should move and shoot the enemies (cubes) inorder to gain points and stay alive. The game is engaging with some extra mechanisms and visuals in a cyberpunk feel.
